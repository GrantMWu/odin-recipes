Spaghetti Recipe

Spaghetti

Mama mia! This spaghetti recipe is a sure fire way to impress at any dinner party or family gathering

Ingredients

  • Spaghetti noodles
  • Pasta Sauce
  • Water

Steps

  1. Boil water
  2. Put noodles into boiling water for 10 minutes or until Al Dente
  3. Drain noodles reserving some pasta water on the side
  4. Mix Spaghetti with pasta sauce and some of the pasta water
